About Us

MASCOT ENGINEERING

We “MASCOT ENGINEERING” are based at Jaipur, dealing with the industrial segment all over the Rajasthan. To us, being a leader not only means selling the best quality products but, it also means working hard to understand needs industries & provide solutions. We came into existence in the field of Compressed air systems and Pneumatics accessories to enhance customer’s productivity & provide energy saving with the most beneficial solutions. We have an expertise in providing best of sales and after sales support and a wide range of products to meet our customer’s need. We have a wide presence and we are catering to various industries (i.e. Cement Plants, Textile Mills, Engineering Industries, OEMs etc.) as solution provider and the commitment to support customers as proudly as we stand behind our product offerings.
